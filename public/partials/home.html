Community Guidelines

  • Create as many stories as you would like, but you can only check out one at a time.
  • Check out a story for a maximum of 24 hours. After the alotted time limit, the story will automatically be unlocked and your unsaved work will be lost.
  • You may not check out a story two consecutive times unless there is a 24 hour period between checkouts.
  • A story is locked while in edit mode and remains locked until the story owner approves or rejects pending contributions. The owner does not have a time limit to approve the contribution.
  • If the genre has been specified, try to adhere to it, even loosely. The story owner has the right to reject your contribution for any reason.
  • Don't be an ass. You can be banned.
